WHAT IS Eloquentica ?
The idea of Eloquentica is to create a database of eloquentive (not casual) words with their synonyms.

HOW TO SEARCH FOR WORDS ?
Type a word (or synonym) that you are looking for in the text bar and hit 'Find it!' button. It will look for certain word in first instance, if not found it will look for synonyms. As a final result, all words with matching synonym will be displayed.

HOW TO ADD WORDS ?
Hit 'Add word' button. Provide 'word', 'definition' and 'synonyms'.
IMPORTANT! separate synonyms by ',' comma! If you will not separate them, they will be treated as one word.
